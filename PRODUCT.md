# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Me — the author. This is a personal reference and public showcase, not an audience-facing product. Visitors are developers evaluating which skills to install.

## Product Purpose

A curated, opinionated directory of 38 AI agent skills — recorded workflows and constraints I find useful. The landing page communicates breadth and quality at a glance, then lets visitors dive into individual skills. Success: the page feels deliberate, not templated.

## Brand Personality

Clean, Technical, Minimal. Dev-tool aesthetic, utilitarian, no decoration for its own sake. Every element earns its place.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing page templates (hero-metric template, numbered section markers, tiny uppercase kickers on every section)
- Over-designed "AI" aesthetics (gradient text, glassmorphism, excessive rounded corners)
- Sparse, template-driven content that undermines the curated premise

## Design Principles

1. **Earn your place** — No decoration, no filler. Every section, every line exists because it carries information.
2. **Practice what you preach** — This page documents skills for agents; its own design should feel considered, deliberate, and technically sound.
3. **Utility over spectacle** — Clean hierarchy, readable typography, sufficient contrast. Let the content (the skills themselves) lead.
4. **Commit to the register** — This is a brand surface. No dashboard tropes, no app-shell patterns.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA as baseline (4.5:1 body contrast, sufficient focus indicators)
- Respect reduced motion
- Functional without JavaScript where possible
